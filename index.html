Who created the Billies?

CryptoBillies is the genesis project of 0xShades and Kiki - you might know us from the Alien Frens community! 👽

We're a husband/wife duo with backgrounds in music and art - join us as we explore the magical world of Web 3, making loads of frens along the way. 😀

CryptoBillies as a project represents and supports our endless love for animals 🌍

We're inspired by the CryptoPunks and CryptoRayRays 🖖

We're inspired by the animals we've loved and lost along the way 🐶

We're giving away 25% of this projects mint revenues to animal charities ⭐

We're also giving away 50% of all secondary sales royalties to animal charities - fur-ever 😻

Pawdon me ser, but can you tell me more?

  • Yup. First of all, each Billie is unique!

    We lovingly created many different traits for the Billies, and then used some JavaScript wizardry to combine them into 10,000 unique Billies.

  • There are some ultra-rare 1/1 Billies in the collection

    These Billies are unique and don't have any shared traits with other Billies. Legend has it that they have their own secretive club in a Discord server somewhere...

  • CryptoBillies are CC0 frenly

    This means that if you own a Billie you can use your Billie in any commercial or non-commercial project. The art is truly yours to do whatever you want with!

  • The Billies can be added to your current PFP as a pet

    Billie backgrounds are all magical and transparent so you can arrange them on top of your current profile picture as pets. Very cool!

Are roadmaps still a thing?

Our roadmap is simple and to the point. Some might even call it purrfect...

  • 1

    At 50% Billies adopted...

    25% of total revenues collected are donated to charities doing pawsome work with animals.

    The CryptoBillies community is presented with a list of charities and we collectively vote on where the donations go.

    If the charities we donate to are interested, we also airdrop reserved Billies to them and invite them to join our community!

  • 2

    At 100% Billies adopted...

    Once again, we collect 25% of our total revenues and donate the funds to trustworthy charities doing clawsome work with animals.

  • 3

    Infinite Donation Loop ∞

    50% of all secondary royalties are routed to a community donation wallet right as adoption begins.

    What this means is that every time a Billie is traded on secondary markets, a percentage of the transaction amount will be routed to animal charities.

    The CryptoBillies community will choose which animal charities to donate the funds to every 4 months!
